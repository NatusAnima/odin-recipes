Roasted Potatoes Recipe

Oven roasted potatoes with spices, garlic and oil.

Ingredients:

  • 6 potatoes, cut into chunks
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of seasonings (Paprika, Oregano ect.)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon cracked black pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees C.
  2. Rinse and dry potatoes with a paper towel and transfer to a large bowl. Add olive oil, seasonings, salt, and black pepper; mix well. Spread potatoes on a baking sheet.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, turning potatoes occasionally. Continue baking until potatoes are tender and golden brown, about 30 minutes more.
